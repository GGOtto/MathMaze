trainer::The Trainer offers an easy way to drill AMC problems. You can chose the mode you want to play in below!
You can solve random problems or try your hand in exam format. Use the help tag if you are confused.
===
free::Free Solve Mode is so you can
continually focus completely on
solving problems.
===
quiz::Exam Mode is so you can solve
an exam with similar rules
to a real AMC. You may choose
any exam, or run it with random
problems.
===
maze::Go through the maze and solve the problems to collect chests. You win when you have collected the 
last chest. Watch out for the creatures in the maze! If anything confuses you, use the help tab.
